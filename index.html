Our Mission

AIO is a Christian organization committed to demostrate the unconditional love of God to all vulnerable people regardless of gender, race or religion through a holistic approach to alleviate human suffering.!

Our Vision

Our vision is a community in which vulnerable households(Women, Youth and Children) are holistically empowered to live a resilient and sustainable livelihood and enjoy dignified life!
